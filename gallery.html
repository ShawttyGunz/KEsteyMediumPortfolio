The still art

Set Me On Fire

Heavily inspired by the track "Set Me On Fire" by the Australian drum&bass band Pendulum. Set to represent the heavy electronic feel with square pixelated objects and flame. There will be an update coming of this in the future. Created in Adobe Photoshop. September 13, 2013.

Under Starry Skies

A piece done for a close friend of mine on Deviantart that had a birthday. This however is between our characters that we use in roleplays (lame, I know?). Created in Paint Tool SAI. November 26, 2013.

9 K M

Another piece inspired by Pendulum. Mainly however this was an experiment of using Photoshop as a painting tool. It was a hell of a lot of strokes and tons of time to finish, but considering its my first real attempt of landscape with brush I was rather satisfied. Created in Adobe Photoshop. August 13, 2013.

Lunar Nova

This was about an hour's work of painting after stopping at the side of the road in the country at 2 AM. Its not often I do brush works but I felt this was a pretty good attempt for how quickly I performed it. Created in Paint Tool SAI. March 17, 2014.

Warm Morning

This was a request I did for a friend after myself and them felt this would be a good scene to recreate after a good bout of roleplay together(And yes, I am still lame for RP). My first major project in Paint Tool SAI. May 21, 2013.

Etheral

Yet another musically inspired piece of artwork, primarily inspired by Svefn-g-englar - Sigur Ros. Mainly focused on a sort of out of real look also based on the live performances in their release of Inni. I will probably be taking on this style more often later. Created in Paint Tool SAI. June 11, 2013.
